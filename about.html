EASTERN AFRICA HEALTH SUMMIT 2023

In Africa, the second most populous continent with over 1.2 billion people, healthcare has huge unmet needs. While infectious diseases continue to exact a terrible toll across many African countries, non-communicable diseases are poised to become the leading causes of death in sub-Saharan Africa by 2030. Africa's people have the greatest genetic diversity on the planet, meaning innovative treatments must tailor to African needs. Africa's 54 countries present hugely diverse challenges in health delivery. The IQVIA Africa Health Summit is a complimentary in-person event which brings together inspiring clinical research and public health experts to share knowledge and exchange ideas about how together we can advance Africa's health through data, technology and innovative research. Join leaders in life sciences companies and decision-makers in the public and private health space striving for broad and equitable access to healthcare.
