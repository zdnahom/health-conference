Eastern Africa Health Summit 2023

In Africa, the second most populous continent with over 1.2 billion people, healthcare has huge unmet needs. While infectious diseases continue to exact a terrible toll across many African countries, non-communicable diseases are poised to become the leading causes of death in sub-Saharan Africa by 2030.
